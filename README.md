# **College Chronicle**

**College Chronicle** is a centralized platform built with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) to streamline communication and collaboration among students, faculty, and college administration. This platform enables registered users such as club coordinators, faculty, HODs, and the principal to post various types of information, making it easily accessible to students.

---

## **Features**

- **Pre-Registered Users**:
  - All users (students, coordinators like faculty, HODs, and all other higher authorities) are pre-registered and authenticated.

- **Content Posting**:
  - **College Activities**: Share updates on academic and extracurricular activities.
  - **Events**: Post details about hackathons, competitions, and training programs.
  - **Achievements**: Highlight student and faculty accomplishments.
  - **Placement Details**: Publish job, internship, and placement opportunities.
  - **Notifications**: Share information on interviews, exams, schedules, circulars, and more.

- **Student Interaction**:
  - **Registration**: Students can directly register for events, training, and other activities.
  - **Tracking**: Monitor exam schedules, circulars, and other important notifications.

- **Search and Filter**:
  - Quickly find specific information using filters and search by category or keywords.

---

## **Tech Stack**

- **Frontend**: React.js
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js
- **Database**: MongoDB (Atlas)
